From President's Pen ...

Sai Ram!

A lot of planning and preparation is underway to get ready for the upcoming Freeze Fun this Saturday, the Family Day Weekend! Whereas the young adults are arranging for on-the-course activities, several parents and Gurus are making sure enough snacks and hot drinks are available for all participants. The weather forecast is calling for mild temperature with light snow which will be ideal for tobogganing etc. With that, I am certain we will have very good participation. The Fun starts at 1.00pm; I am sure you would not want to miss this once-a-year event to socialize outdoors. Please look for the details of location and directions in this newsletter.

A very ambitious program is being developed for the upcoming Maha Shivrathri. An elaborate Linga Abhishekham, amidst chanting of Sri Rudram and other Vedic Hymns is being planned. Please check the poster for details. This will be our first day of the Centre opening after a long break. Though most restrictions on size of gatherings etc. would have been lifted by the province by then, we will still follow basic guidelines to prevent the spread of the Covid-19 virus. Also, with Divine Grace, our Chairlift will be in use by that time; we will announce next week if you will be able test the ride. Completion of the Chairlift project brings in an important item of the Centre clean-up! The contractor has promised to do some basic clean-up, but we need several volunteers between Feb 21-Feb 26 to clean up the Centre and make it worthy of prayers on Maha Shivrathri. Please get in touch with brother Anshuman anshumantyagi@hotmail.com or sister Vishnu vsoobiah@gmail.com, if you could devote a few hours for the Centre cleanup during next week.

I extend my hearty congratulations to the three members, sisters Kuha Yogan and Bhagya Venkatraman, and brother Naga Devarakonda who successfully completed a very strenuous Gurusâ€™ training this week. Depending upon their availability, they will fill the urgent need of the Centre for trained Gurus. Being a Guru is one of the most selfless and rewarding services one could offer to our fellow members and children. So, please get in touch with our SSE coordinator, sister Jaya jayaedmsse@gmail.com for more information on future training sessions.

We are very closely following Provincial announcements regarding lifting of various restrictions related to Covid-19. In case the Covid-19 affected cases decline significantly, we will plan to open the Centre for regular Bhajans when considered safe. So, please stay tuned for the future announcements through this newsletter.

With loving Sairam,

Prem Kharbanda
