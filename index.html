Thought for the Week
"All that relates to the body is temporary and transient. What one should seek is enduring bliss. Happiness is union with God. Buddha prescribed five duties: good vision, good thoughts, listening to good things, good speech, and good actions. These five-fold duties constitute true spiritual practice (sadhana)."
— Sri Sathya Sai Baba, May 11, 1998
